## Target identity
- Public recruiting intermediary: TalentPluto / Pluto.
- Confidential hiring partner: not identified in the supplied posting and not guessed.
- Candidate context: independent candidate vision for a TalentPluto-managed search.

## Official and supplied sources
- https://talentpluto.com/
- https://talentpluto.com/companies
- https://www.ycombinator.com/companies/talentpluto
- `assets/brand/talentpluto-logo.svg` — user-supplied campaign asset, visually consistent with the blue circular/four-point-star mark shown on Pluto's public Y Combinator profile.

## Visible identity decision
The supplied TalentPluto mark appears above the fold, unaltered, beside the explicit qualifier `Independent candidate vision by Russell Dudek`. It is used only for nominative identification and does not imply sponsorship or endorsement. The campaign does not recreate, recolor, crop, animate, or merge the mark into a candidate-created logo.

## Public visual grammar observed
- warm off-white / cream field
- high-contrast black editorial typography
- oversized editorial sans-serif headlines
- playful but restrained rounded surfaces
- conversational UI and phone / message motifs
- direct, low-friction copy emphasizing context, privacy, and warm introductions

## Typography
The official public typeface could not be verified from a licensable downloadable source. The campaign uses Trebuchet MS for rounded editorial display behavior and Arial for broad PDF/browser reliability. These are documented substitutes, not claimed official fonts.

## Role-derived visual and motion system
The signature visual is the Client Delta Atlas: three enterprise account layers align over a reusable product core. Scenario selection changes the disposition, evidence requirement, layer state, semantic labels, and ARIA state as one transaction. Motion runs once and settles; reduced motion resolves immediately.

## Artifact application
- Site: supplied mark, public-site warm field, sampled blue recognition layer, original Client Delta Atlas.
- Resume and cover letter: restrained blue rules and a visible TalentPluto-managed role line; no fake corporate letterhead.
- Interview brief, entry plan, and operating brief: the same blue/state grammar and independent-candidate framing.

## Independent-candidate distinction
Every public surface identifies the work as Russell Dudek's independent candidate vision and states that it is not affiliated with or endorsed by Pluto or the confidential partner.
