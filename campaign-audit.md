## User-directed document and geometry correction

The document routes previously opened browser PDF panes rather than presenting readable online documents. That made the experience visually inconsistent with the stronger campaign sites and allowed the cover letter to appear blank when the browser's PDF plug-in failed. The hero caption was also positioned over the lower Prototype / Proof card.

The approved correction:

- replaces all five PDF-viewer wrappers with responsive, branded HTML documents;
- preserves native PDF downloads and exact `2 / 1 / 2 / 2 / 2` print pagination;
- keeps Resume and Cover Letter reciprocal navigation;
- renders the full cover letter as visible HTML and verifies nonblank extracted PDF text;
- places the hero caption in normal layout flow with a measured minimum clearance from the Prototype / Proof card;
- preserves the approved blue/white client-to-product visual direction, thesis, section order, and interaction concept.
