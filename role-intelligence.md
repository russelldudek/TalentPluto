# Role intelligence

## Nominal role
Strategy & Operations Associate for a TalentPluto-managed confidential AI search software company.

## Real mandate
Act as a forward-deployed product operator: manage enterprise accounts, translate customization into product requirements, build production-minded prototypes and agents, and create engineering handoffs that improve both client outcomes and platform leverage.

## Central tensions
1. Enterprise specificity vs. platform coherence.
2. Prototype speed vs. engineering confidence.
3. Founder-led judgment vs. scalable operating memory.
4. Immediate client delivery vs. reusable roadmap value.

## Likely success measures
- Enterprise onboarding and time-to-value
- Measurable client result
- Prototype learning speed and handoff quality
- Reusable capability created from account work
- Reduced rework and custom-support burden
- Clear roadmap decisions and ownership

## Strongest plausible objection
Russell's experience is broader and more senior than the nominal title suggests. The campaign answers this by leading with direct execution, building, client translation, and willingness to own the work itself. Scope calibration remains an interview discovery topic rather than a public self-disqualification.

## Candidate thesis
Turn enterprise customization into a compounding product advantage by separating the shared core from legitimate client deltas, proving behavior quickly, and returning validated learning to the roadmap.
